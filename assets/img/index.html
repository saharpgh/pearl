EXPLORE INFINITE POTENTIAL

Rootfood is a personalized Nutritional Advisor

and Private Chef Service aimed at helping you

explore the infinite possibilities of a delicious,

well balanced, carefully evaluated diet.

A CHOICE TO ENJOY

At Rootfood we are convinced that a creative stimulation of your

flavor receptors can inspire and sustain change in all aspects of life,

no matter your current health status, age, level of physical activity or

profession.

GIULIU URBANI, CHEF, NUTRITIONIST AND CONCEPT DEVELOPER



“To me, cooking is part of an ongoing journey of self-discovery

that puts me in touch with the cycles of nature, and gives me the

chance to express my creativity whilst still following my

inspiration. it is along this path that I've come to understand

that my interest is not merely on the aesthetics of food; I'm

especially interested in its essence: the deeper bond between

mankind and its surroundings. Rootfood comes as a result of

such a process of growth and self-realization."

WHO



THE CHEF

Giulio Urbani has worked for over 10 years with international Chefs,

and his experience has been shaped by a multitude of kitchens, from

Michelin star restaurants to catering and private events, big hotels

and small private venues, which gave him the opportunity to cater to

HNW and UHNW clients all over the world.

THE NUTRITIONIST

In order to deepen his knowledge and understanding of the human
body in relation to food, Giulio Urbani has completed a MSc in
Human Nutrition and Applied Dietetics held by the prestigious
Università Politecnica delle Marche.
